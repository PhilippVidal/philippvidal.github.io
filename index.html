Projects

Avoiding Motion Sickness in VR

As part of my master's thesis, I developed a virtual reality application that offers an interactive VR environment designed for testing different approaches to reduce and avoid motion sickness. This application was used to conduct a small-scale research study to evaluate the effect of different locomotion methods and user interaction on the occurance of the malady within users.

Serious Game - Compulsive Hoarding

Our group of seven students collaborated on a VR serious game aimed at helping individuals struggling with compulsive hoarding. We worked closely with a team of psychologists to ensure that the game met their specific criteria and requirements for therapy purposes. The game's immersive VR environment allowed users to experience scenarios related to their hoarding behavior in a safe and controlled setting, providing them with the opportunity to develop coping strategies and skills.

Procedurally generated vegetation in AR

As part of my bachelor's thesis, I developed an augmented reality application that leverages the Spatial Mapping feature of Microsoft's HoloLens 2 to create an immersive and interactive experience for the user. Using procedural generation, the application is capable of generating different types of vegetation, enabling the user to envision their real-world environment in an overgrown state.

Mod - More Explosives

A mod for the game DayZ that adds multiple types of explosives to the game. These explosives can be triggered via a timer or remotely by using a detonator. Each detonator can be connected to multiple explosives at once, with the list of connected explosives being replicated efficiently over the network. Similarly to my other DayZ mod "Breachingcharge", this mod also features a base raiding system that can be configured by the user.

Mod - Breachingcharge

My first mod for the game DayZ that adds a configurable base raiding system. Building bases and raiding other's is a big part of the base game and the main focus for many players, especially on modded servers. This mod was developed to create a more enjoyable raiding experience for players and provide the server owner with more options to configure compared to the raiding system of the base game.
