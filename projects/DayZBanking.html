Banking - DayZ Mod

  • Github
  • Steam

A mod that adds a banking system to DayZ. It is designed to add a level of cross-progression to the game that rewards players for storing their excess in-game currency, allowing them to gain wealth and progression in the game regardless of whether they die or their base gets raided.

What was my role?

While my brother is the main developer of this mod, I've helped out in various areas during its creation and assisted with fixes throughout its lifetime. This was one of the first mods either of us had created for the game and both of us had much to learn. Sharing our existing knowledge led to me being involved in the mod's code, design, and QA testing, as well as providing help with setting up the in-game models and importing them into the game.
