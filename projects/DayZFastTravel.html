Fast Travel - DayZ Mod

  • Github
  • Steam

A mod that adds a fast travel system to DayZ which allows players to teleport between a set of predefined locations. DayZ is often described as a "walking simulator" due to the vast distances players have to travel to get to their destination and it's not uncommon to go hours without seeing another player. This mod aims to not only make it easier for players to meet up with their friends, but also to make remote areas more accessible, encouraging the exploration of otherwise underutilized locations.

While I'm not the main developer of this mod, I've helped with the code, design and QA testing throughout its creation.
